About the Group
Built on two continents. Driven by one purpose.

Data Vision Tech Solutions operates as two complementary entities — the UK-registered procurement arm in Birmingham and RA Recycle Service, our processing facility in Rajasthan, India.

Data Vision Tech Solutions was incorporated in Birmingham in August 2025 as the UK procurement arm — licensed by the Environment Agency as an upper-tier waste carrier and registered with HMRC for cross-border export.

RA Recycle Service was established in Kuchera, Nagaur, Rajasthan. It holds a DGFT Import-Export Code, GST registration, ISO 9001:2015 certification, and RSPCB Consent to Operate valid through February 2035 at 2,500 MT/month licensed capacity. Visit the facility online at rarecycleservice.com.

FAQ
Direct answers to the questions that matter.
For UK Scrap Suppliers

Are you a licensed waste carrier?

+
Yes. We hold UK Environment Agency Waste Carrier Licence CBDU622654 as an upper-tier registered waste carrier. Verifiable on the Environment Agency's public register.

What types of metal do you buy?

+
We buy all grades of ferrous and non-ferrous metal — steel and cast iron, copper, brass, aluminium, stainless steel, lead, and cable. We also handle complete factory clearances, end-of-life vehicles, and redundant plant & machinery. Contact our Birmingham office to discuss your material.

How do you price the material?

+
Pricing is based on material type, grade, and current market rates. We conduct an in-field assessment before confirming a price in writing. Contact us for current indicative pricing.

Do you provide waste transfer documentation?

+
Yes. As a licensed upper-tier waste carrier, we issue waste transfer notes for every collection as required by UK regulations. Documentation is provided at the time of collection.

Do you handle factory clearances and end-of-life vehicles?

+
Yes. We clear factories, workshops, and industrial sites in full — removing redundant metal, plant, and machinery — and we take in end-of-life vehicles for depollution and recycling. We collect nationwide and handle larger jobs on a scheduled basis.

Where are you based and do you collect across the UK?

+
Our UK office is at 1082 Stratford Road, Hall Green, Birmingham B28 8AD. We buy scrap metal and collect from suppliers nationwide across England and Wales, with larger loads scheduled in advance.

How do I sell my scrap metal to you?

+
Contact our Birmingham office by phone or through the enquiry form with a description of your material and a rough quantity. We give you an indicative price, arrange collection or drop-off, weigh and grade the material on site, and issue a waste transfer note with payment.
For Global Buyers

Is RA Recycle Service registered to export?

+
Yes. RA Recycle Service holds DGFT Import-Exporter Code DVBPR7915G, the mandatory Government of India registration for international trade. Verifiable at dgft.gov.in.

What is the licensed processing capacity?

+
2,500 metric tonnes per month, as specified in RSPCB Consent to Operate Order 2025-2026/Nagour/14384, valid through February 2035.

Is the facility ISO certified?

+
Yes. ISO 9001:2015 certified. Certificate UACB/R2502/104, scope: Importer & Trader of Metal Scrap. Valid to 18 April 2028.

What payment terms do you accept?

+
We work with Letter of Credit (LC), Telegraphic Transfer (TT), and other standard trade finance instruments. Terms are agreed directly based on order volume.
