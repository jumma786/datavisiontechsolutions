Your Protection

Duty Of Care & Documentation

Under UK environmental law, every business that produces waste has a duty of care to ensure it is passed only to an authorised carrier and handled responsibly. That responsibility doesn't stop at your gate — which is why using a licensed carrier and keeping the right paperwork matters.

With every collection you receive:

  • A Waste Transfer Note recording the material, quantity and parties involved.
  • Weighbridge tickets documenting the weights your payment is based on.
  • A clear payment statement tied to those weights and agreed prices.

Together these give you a complete, auditable record that your metal was handled legally and recycled responsibly. Read our guide to waste transfer notes & duty of care →

FAQs

Compliance Questions

Is Data Vision Tech Solutions a licensed waste carrier?
Yes. We're registered with the Environment Agency as an upper-tier waste carrier, broker and dealer under registration CBDU622654, verifiable on the EA public register.

What documentation do I receive when you collect my scrap?
Every collection comes with a waste transfer note, weighbridge tickets and a clear payment statement — your evidence of compliant disposal under duty of care.

What is duty of care and how does it affect me?
Under UK law, any business that produces waste must ensure it's passed only to an authorised carrier and handled correctly. Using a licensed carrier and keeping the waste transfer note protects you, because liability doesn't end at your gate.
