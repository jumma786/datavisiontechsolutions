Who We Are

Sourcing In The UK, Recycling For The World

Data Vision Tech Solutions Pvt Limited is a licensed scrap metal buyer, processor and exporter based in Hall Green, Birmingham. We buy ferrous and non-ferrous scrap from suppliers, sites and clearances across the UK, then sort, grade and process it for export to industrial buyers and foundries worldwide.

What sets us apart is that we own the whole chain — UK sourcing paired with cost-efficient, government-licensed processing capacity means better value for suppliers and reliable, traceable supply for buyers.
