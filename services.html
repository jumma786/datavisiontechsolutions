Scrap metal services, end to end

One licensed operation covering sourcing, clearance, processing and export — with full documentation at every stage.

Scrap Metal Sourcing

We buy ferrous and non-ferrous scrap from trade, industry and the public at competitive, transparent prices. Sorted or mixed, small loads or bulk — we weigh accurately and pay promptly.

Factory & Site Clearance

Fully licensed clearance of factories and industrial sites, from first survey to the final weighbridge ticket — scheduled around your operations with complete paperwork.

Nationwide Collection

Free collection across Birmingham and the West Midlands for qualifying loads, with UK-wide collection available for larger commercial quantities.

End-of-Life Vehicles

Compliant collection and processing of end-of-life vehicles and vehicle salvage, handled to environmental standards with the right documentation.

Plant & Machinery

Safe isolation, dismantling and removal of redundant plant and heavy machinery, recovering maximum value from end-of-life assets.

Sorting & Grading

Material separated by metal type and grade — ferrous, copper, aluminium, brass, stainless, cable and more — for clean, consistent output.

Processing & Compaction

Baling, shredding and compaction to foundry-grade stock, improving handling efficiency and value through our processing facility.

Export Logistics

Documented container logistics and global export of processed metal to industrial buyers and foundries worldwide.

How It Works

Our process, step by step

A clear, documented path from first contact to payment and export.

1

Enquiry & survey

Tell us what you have — by phone, form, or a quick photo. For clearances we arrange a site survey to assess metals, grades, access and hazards.

2

Quotation

You get a clear quote: what we'll pay for the recoverable metal and how the work will be carried out. No guesswork, no hidden deductions.

3

Collection & removal

We collect — free across the West Midlands for qualifying loads — and safely disconnect and remove plant and machinery where needed.

4

Sorting & weighing

Material is separated by grade and weighed transparently on auditable scales — the weights that determine your payment.

5

Documentation & payment

You receive waste transfer notes, weighbridge tickets and prompt payment — your evidence of fully compliant, legal disposal.
