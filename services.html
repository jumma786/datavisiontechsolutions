Service

Scrap Metal Buying

We buy all grades of ferrous and non-ferrous metal — aluminium, copper, brass, stainless, lead, cable and steel — from the public, trade and industry. Sorted or mixed, a single load or regular tonnage, we'll quote it fairly.

How it works: tell us what you have by phone, WhatsApp photo or our online form. We give you a price based on the current market and the grade of your metal, then arrange payment on collection or drop-off.

Why us: transparent, auditable weighing and clear pricing tied to live market rates — no hidden deductions, prompt payment, and full documentation every time.

Service

Free Collection

We offer free collection across Birmingham and the West Midlands for qualifying loads, with nationwide collection available for larger commercial quantities.

How it works: book a slot that suits you; our team arrives with the right vehicle and equipment, loads safely, weighs transparently and pays you with the correct paperwork.

Ideal for: households, tradespeople, garages, builders and businesses who want hassle-free removal without transporting metal themselves.

Service

Factory & Site Clearance

Full, fully licensed clearance of factories and industrial sites — from a single production line to a complete strip-out — handled from first survey to the final weighbridge ticket.

How it works: we survey the site, assess the metals, machinery, access and hazards, then schedule the clearance around your operations, staging larger jobs over several collections.

You receive: waste transfer notes, weighbridge tickets and a clear payment statement — your evidence the waste was handled legally under duty of care.

Service

End-of-Life Vehicles (ELVs)

Compliant collection and processing of cars, vans and end-of-life vehicles, plus vehicle salvage and mixed vehicle metal.

How it works: we collect the vehicle and ensure it is processed through authorised channels with the correct documentation, including DVLA notification. Have the V5C/logbook ready to speed things up.

Service

Plant & Machinery Removal

Safe isolation, dismantling and removal of redundant industrial plant, manufacturing machinery, motors and transformers — recovering maximum value from end-of-life assets.

How it works: we assess what needs disconnecting and dismantling, bring the right lifting and cutting equipment, and remove everything safely while protecting the parts of your site you're keeping.

Service

Sorting & Grading

Recovered material is separated by metal type and grade — ferrous, copper, aluminium, brass, stainless, cable and more — so every tonne is clean, consistent and correctly valued.

Accurate grading is what determines a fair price, which is why we sort and weigh transparently rather than estimating.

Service

Processing & Compaction

Through our ISO 9001:2015 certified facility we bale, shred and compact material into clean, foundry-ready stock, improving handling efficiency and value.

Our government-licensed capacity of 2,500 MT/month lets us process volume reliably and to a consistent standard.

Service

Export Logistics

We handle documented container logistics and global export of processed metal to industrial buyers and foundries worldwide, backed by IEC, EORI and customs documentation.

Owning the full chain — UK sourcing through processing to export — gives buyers reliable, traceable supply and full chain of custody.

Service

Trade & Account Supply

Regular collections and account arrangements for builders, fabricators, manufacturers and trade suppliers who generate scrap on an ongoing basis.

We can supply separate containers, schedule routine pickups and provide consolidated paperwork to make recurring disposal simple.
