This Privacy Policy explains how Data Vision Tech Solutions Pvt Limited ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use our website or contact us. We are the data controller for the purposes of UK data protection law (the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

Who we are

Data Vision Tech Solutions Pvt Limited, a company registered in England & Wales (company number 16681514), registered office 1082 Stratford Road, Hall Green, Birmingham, B28 8AD, United Kingdom. Environment Agency upper-tier waste carrier registration CBDU622654.

What data we collect

When you submit our enquiry form or contact us directly, we collect:

  • Your name and company name
  • Your email address and phone number
  • Details of your enquiry (role, material, estimated quantity, and message)

We do not knowingly collect any special category data. Please do not include sensitive personal information in your enquiry.

How we use it, and our lawful basis

We use this information to respond to your enquiry, provide quotes, and carry out our business of buying, processing, and supplying scrap metal. Our lawful basis is our legitimate interests in responding to business enquiries and, where applicable, taking steps to enter into a contract with you.

Who we share it with

  • Web3Forms — our enquiry form is processed by Web3Forms, which delivers your submission to our email inbox. See their privacy policy at web3forms.com.
  • Chat assistant — the chat assistant on our site runs entirely within your own web browser. The messages you type are not sent to us or to any third party and are not stored once you close the page. To get a quote or share details with us, please call, WhatsApp, email, or use our enquiry form.
  • Our email provider, used to receive and store correspondence.
  • We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data only as long as necessary to deal with your enquiry and any resulting business relationship, and to meet our legal and accounting obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, or object to our processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email info@datavisiontechsolutions.com.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate this website and, with your consent, to understand how it is used and to support our marketing. Cookies fall into three categories:

  • Essential — always active. Required for the site to function, including remembering your cookie choices and enabling the contact form. These do not track you.
  • Analytics — optional. With your consent we use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymous, aggregated statistics about how visitors use the site so we can improve it.
  • Marketing — optional. With your consent we may use advertising tools (such as the Meta Pixel) to measure and improve our advertising.

When you first visit, we ask for your consent before setting any analytics or marketing cookies. You can accept all, reject all, or choose individual categories. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by clicking “Cookie settings” in the footer of any page. We also load fonts from Google Fonts to display the site correctly.
